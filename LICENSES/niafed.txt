NIAFED — National Indicative Aggregated Fire Extent Dataset

Source: Australian Government Department of Climate Change, Energy, the
Environment and Water (DCCEEW). Aggregated from state agency fire footprints
for the 2019–2020 season.

Landing page:
https://fed.dcceew.gov.au/datasets/national-indicative-aggregated-fire-extent-dataset

This repository uses NIAFED to refine AOI polygons from their initial
Wikipedia-derived bounding boxes to the actual 2019-2020 fire extent
(planned in milestone M3 refinement work).
