IMPORTANT CAVEAT (propagated from upstream documentation):
- AUS GEEBAM classes are not based on field data.
- Low and moderate severity are combined into a single class upstream.
- Published comparisons against state products are NOT traditional validation —
  there is no true point-of-truth dataset.
- This repository treats GEEBAM as a PUBLIC PROXY LABEL SOURCE, not ground truth.
